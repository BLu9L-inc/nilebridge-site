Common Questions

Answers to What
We Hear Most.

QWhat exactly is the Automotive Intelligence Grid?
The AIG is intelligence infrastructure for automotive dealers — not a CRM, not a DMS add-on. It captures every operational event inside a dealership, resolves those events into a living knowledge graph, and surfaces decision intelligence for sales managers, GMs, used vehicle buyers, and F&I teams. Every outcome retrains the system, so the platform compounds in value the longer it runs.
QIs the system actually live right now?
Yes. The AIG is fully operational. We are running a full pilot with real dealership partners — real inventory, real transactions, real outcomes feeding back into the system. August 2026 is the public launch date, when we open access broadly.
QWhat makes AIG different from existing dealer tools?
Existing dealer tools capture data. AIG converts data into organizational knowledge — a closed learning loop where every sale, every miss, and every market movement retrains the system. The result is not a report. It is a continuously improving intelligence layer that gets sharper with every event it processes.
QWhich departments does AIG serve?
AIG surfaces intelligence across the entire dealership — used vehicle buying, pricing, inventory turn, F&I performance, sales velocity, and service throughput. Every department generates events; every department receives intelligence. The platform is designed so each role sees what is most relevant to their decisions.
QHow does the Founding Dealer program work?
Dealers who join before the August 2026 public launch lock in founding-tier positioning and pricing. Capacity is intentionally limited. Founding dealers get earliest access to the intelligence layer, input into platform development, and terms that are not available post-launch.
QWho is blu9L Holdings?
blu9L is a cross-border intelligence holding company operating across the United States and UAE. Founded and led by Yaseen Hassan, blu9L builds intelligence infrastructure businesses — starting with the Automotive Intelligence Grid as the first deployed vertical.
QWhat is the UAE Advisory practice?
Separately from AIG, blu9L operates a licensed UAE advisory practice — investment advisory, commercial brokerage, and transaction facilitation across the Gulf, US, and Africa corridor. This practice serves institutions entering the Gulf, operators seeking UAE market access, and principals in cross-border transactions.

Use the contact form below, email yaseen.hassan@blu9l.com, or reach out via sales.force@blu9l.com for dealer-specific enquiries. We respond to every genuine enquiry within one business day.
