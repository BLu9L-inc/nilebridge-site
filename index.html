Common Questions

Answers.

QWhat exactly is the Automotive Intelligence Grid?
Intelligence infrastructure for dealers — not a CRM or DMS add-on. The AIG captures every operational event, resolves it into a living knowledge graph, and surfaces decision intelligence across every department. Every outcome retrains the system.
QIs the system actually live right now?
Yes. Fully operational with a full pilot running with real dealership partners today — real inventory, real transactions, real outcomes feeding back in. August 2026 is the public launch date.
QWhat makes AIG different from existing dealer tools?
Existing tools capture data. AIG converts data into organizational knowledge — a closed learning loop where every sale, every miss, and every market movement retrains the system. It gets sharper the more events it processes.
QHow does the Founding Dealer program work?
Dealers who join before August 2026 lock in founding-tier positioning and pricing. Capacity is intentionally limited. Founding dealers get earliest access, input into development, and terms not available post-launch.
QWho is BLu9L Holdings?
A cross-border intelligence holding company operating across the US and UAE. Founded and led by Yaseen Hassan, BLu9L builds intelligence infrastructure businesses — starting with the Automotive Intelligence Grid as the first deployed vertical.
QHow do I get access?
Use the contact form below, email info@blu9l.com, or reach sales.force@blu9l.com for dealer-specific enquiries. We respond to every genuine enquiry within one business day.
