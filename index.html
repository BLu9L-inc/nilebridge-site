Common Questions

Answers to the
Questions We Hear Most.

What is IRAS®?
IRAS® (Intelligent Recognition and Analytics System) is an AI-powered school intelligence platform built on IoT hardware. It automates attendance, monitors campus safety in real time, and connects parents to their child's school day — without manual data entry from staff. Live in 22 UAE schools since 2025.
Why Blu9L?
Blu9L holds the exclusive IRAS® franchise for Egypt — the only company authorised to deploy IRAS® across the country. Backed by a formal agreement with Neuronso Technology (UAE), tri-jurisdictional legal structure, and an on-the-ground Egypt team led by Dr. Amany Moustafa, DBA.
Who is IRAS® for?
IRAS® is designed for private schools across Egypt — A+ and B+ tier institutions. Suitable for single campuses and multi-campus school groups. Education authorities can also access institutional-grade compliance reporting.
How does deployment work?
A full deployment — from initial assessment to live operations — typically takes 4–8 weeks. This includes hardware installation (IoT wearables, gateway servers), platform setup, and staff training. All hardware is supported by Neuronso Technology. No disruption during installation.
How is student data protected?
All student data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is role-based — parents see only their child's data. Compliant with Egyptian Personal Data Protection Law No. 151/2020. No student data is sold or shared.
